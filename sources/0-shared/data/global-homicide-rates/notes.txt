The risk of being murdered has been declining for a quarter of a century. Eastern Europe in particular has seen steep drops, by as much as 75%.

The reasons are hard to pin down, but society is improving in many ways. Greater rule of law. Better offender rehabilitation. Increasing literacy. And, crucially, self discipline. We simply have more respect and self discipline than we used to.
